Ikura

Ikura helps users pay off their credit card debt. Users enter in three simple numbers - debt amount, an annual percentage rate, and the number of months to pay off a credit card - and Ikura does the rest. Ikura provides access to personalized payment plans, informative visualizations, and next steps for users as they work towards becoming debt free.
